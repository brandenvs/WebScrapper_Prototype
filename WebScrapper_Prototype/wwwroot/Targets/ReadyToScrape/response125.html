    I'm convinced this monitor is the best bang for buck 1440p high refresh option available in the country. I spent DAYS researching our available options and nothing seems to beat this one for pixel response time alongside all the other features. Even at it's regular price, you get a fantastic vibrant IPS panel with 165hz and freesync. G-sync does work but I wouldn't say flawlessly - unfortunately using G-sync limits the adaptive refresh range to 60-165hz instead of 48-165hz. This means that every time your GPU drops below 60fps, you feel a noticeable drop in smoothness as the monitor switches to "low frame rate compensation mode" and starts running the monitor refresh rate at double the fps (e.g. 55fps = 110hz). Not a deal breaker and it's worth mentioning that other non-Gsync-certified monitors tend to do the same anyway.

    Otherwise, I haven't noticed any flaws with the panel. There is some minor edge bleed but this is to be expected with IPS panels and I can only see it in very dark rooms. Shouldn't be a problem unless you like to play/watch in the dark (which isn't good for your eyes anyway).

    10/10 would recommend and buy again.
